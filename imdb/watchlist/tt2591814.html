User Reviews

 
Garden of Wonders
7 July 2013 | by Mystikal104 (Canada) – See all my reviews

Having never seen a Makoto Shinkai film before, I decided to give this one a peek, having heard that he is practically "the next Hayao Miyazaki". I can definitely see why. I was enamoured by the film from beginning to end. The visual imagery was extremely superb. Each scene was visually scintillating...so much so that it pretty much puts live cinematography to shame.

The story itself was quite on par as well. A forbidden love story set in the backdrop of Nagoya, Japan's rainy season. The emotional tone was executed just right enough to give your heart that extra beat. All in all, a perfect short film in my books.
